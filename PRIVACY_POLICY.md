# Privacy Policy for Kitchen Drop

**Last updated: July 3, 2026**

Kitchen Drop ("the app", "we", "us") is a cooking-challenge app that helps you build a daily cooking habit, earn XP, and connect with friends. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and what choices you have.

## No account, no email, no password

Kitchen Drop does not require you to sign up with an email address, phone number, or password. When you first open the app, it generates a random device identifier that is used to identify you within the app's social features. We never ask for or store your real name, email, or any other contact information.

## Information we collect

**Stored only on your device** (never leaves your phone unless noted below):
- Your chosen chef code and username
- Your cooking streak, XP, level, badges, and challenge history
- Photos you take or choose to log your dishes with
- App settings, such as your light/dark mode preference and reminder times

**Stored on our servers** (via Supabase, our backend provider), only if you use social features:
- Your device identifier, chef code, and username, so friends can find and recognize you
- Your friend connections and rival selections
- Basic activity you choose to share: challenge theme, tier, self-rating, and XP earned for a given day (used to show friends what you cooked, not the photo itself)
- Recipes you explicitly choose to share with friends (title, ingredients, steps, notes)
- Nudges and shared-challenge invites sent between friends

**Photos are never uploaded.** Any photo you attach to a cook log stays on your device only.

## What we don't do

- We don't sell your data.
- We don't use your data for advertising or ad targeting.
- We don't track you across other apps or websites.
- We don't collect precise location data.

## Permissions the app requests

- **Camera / Photos** — only used if you choose to attach a photo to a cook log; stays on your device.
- **Notifications** — used for daily cooking reminders and streak alerts, which you can turn off in Settings.
- **Microphone** — requested by an underlying camera library but not used by any feature in the app; we do not record or process audio.

## Your choices

- Social features (friends, rivals, shared recipes) are entirely optional — the app works fully offline for personal streak tracking.
- You can remove friend connections or stop sharing recipes at any time from within the app.
- Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored data. To request deletion of your synced social data (chef code, username, friend list, shared activity) from our servers, contact us using the email below with your chef code.

## Children's privacy

Kitchen Drop is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.
